A Word from Grace

Grace Turner

Sanctuary Grace Ministry · Founder

I understand what you're going through — because I've been there.

On November 5, 2024, God broke my ankle. Not to punish me — but to still me. I was drowning. A small SSA check that barely covered anything. Responsibilities that felt impossible. Noise so loud I couldn't hear His voice. Then He made me unable to run.

In that forced stillness, I learned what you're about to discover: the breakthrough you're seeking is waiting in the silence you're avoiding. Six years of God's faithfulness. One broken ankle that revealed it all. What He did for me, He wants to do for you.

— Grace
