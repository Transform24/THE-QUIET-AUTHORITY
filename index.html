Grace's Testimony

November 5, 2024

God broke my ankle. Not to punish me — to still me. I was drowning. Running. Performing for love I already had. Searching in every room, every relationship, every achievement for something that was never going to come from any of them.

In that forced stillness — six years of God's provision made visible. A small check that covered everything it needed to cover. Faithfulness I had been too busy to notice. He didn't break my ankle to punish me. He broke it to give me back to myself.

What He did for me, He wants to do for you. The breakthrough you are seeking is waiting in the silence you have been avoiding.

Your Guide

Grace Turner

Founder · Sanctuary Grace Ministry

I was never the people's pick. Not in rooms. Not in relationships. Not in the places where the world decides who matters and who waits. I know what it is to do everything right and still be overlooked — to perform and strive and try, not just for success, but for attention no one was giving me. For love that only God could provide.

He chose me the way He chose David — still in the field, doing the unseen work. Not because of my position or my performance. Because of my heart posture. That is called unmerited favor. And it undid me completely.

This ministry is my gratitude. He chose me when no one else did. Now I find you. The Quiet Authority is a solution for transformation — one small win at a time. Not another thing to add to your list. A place to be still, be known, and discover what God has been trying to tell you in the silence you have been running from.

"I was not the people's pick. But God chose me. And He chose you too."
