The Guilty Giver — A Profile of Grace

## Pin Description (caption)

She is the one who answers every text. Who shows up with a meal when no one
asked. Who cannot sit down in her own home without folding one more thing,
finishing one more task, holding one more weight that was never hers to carry.

She gives until she is hollow. And when stillness finally reaches for her,
she calls it laziness. She calls it selfish. She calls it anything but
what it is — an invitation.

If this is you, beloved, hear this gently. Your exhaustion is not proof
of your love. It is proof that you have forgotten you, too, are loved.

There is a quieter way. A profile, a practice, a path back to the woman
beneath the giving.

The assessment is free. The stillness is real.
