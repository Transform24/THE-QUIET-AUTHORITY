## Pin Title (Pinterest title field, ≤100 chars)
A Stillness That Heals What Striving Never Could

## Pin Description (caption)

There is a particular ache that lives inside the woman who has been pushing for so long she no longer knows where the pushing ends and she begins.

She has tried harder. She has prayed longer. She has carried more than anyone has thanked her for. And still, the quiet voice inside her keeps whispering that she is not yet enough.

You were not made to strive your way into peace. Peace was never the prize at the end of one more effort. It was always meant to be the ground beneath your feet.

There is a stillness that heals what striving never could. It does not ask you to perform. It does not measure what you have done today. It simply meets you — in the dim kitchen, in the unmade bed, in the moment before anyone else needs you.

You are allowed to stop. You are allowed to be loved before you have earned it.
