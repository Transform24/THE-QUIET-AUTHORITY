## Pin Title (Pinterest title field, ≤100 chars)
The Guilty Giver — A Quiet Word For The Woman Who Cannot Stop Pouring

## Pin Description (caption)

There is a particular kind of exhaustion that comes from giving until your own well runs dry, and then giving still — because the guilt of stopping feels heavier than the weight of carrying it all.

If you have ever sat in the quiet and felt the ache of being the one everyone leans on, the one who answers, the one who fixes, the one who never quite gets to rest — this is for you.

The Guilty Giver is not a failure of love. She is a woman who learned to measure her worth in what she pours out, and forgot that even Jesus withdrew.

There is a stillness that does not require you to earn it. A grace that meets you before the dishes are done.

You are allowed to be filled before you are emptied again.
