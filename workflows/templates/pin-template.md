# Pinterest Pin Template

Use this format for every pin. Copy → Canva for the visual → Post.

---

## Pin Copy Format

**Title** (max 100 chars):
```
[Keyword phrase] | [Emotional hook or question]
```

**Description** (150–300 chars):
```
[Opening hook — speak to pain or desire]
[What they'll get or discover]
[Soft CTA] → [link or "link in bio"]
```

**Link**: https://transform24.github.io/THE-QUIET-AUTHORITY/

**Board**: [choose from boards list in Agent 02]

---

## Pin Visual Specs
- Size: 1000 x 1500 px (2:3 ratio)
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Text overlay: Max 20% of image
- Font: Cormorant Garamond (serif) or Cinzel for headlines
- Colors: Dark background (#0b0b0b), gold text (#c9a96e), cream accent (#f0ead8)

---

## Brand Voice Reminders
- Quiet, sacred, prophetic — never loud or pushy
- Speak TO the woman's inner experience
- Questions work better than statements
- Words that resonate: stillness, sacred, breakthrough, authority, silence, holy, rest
