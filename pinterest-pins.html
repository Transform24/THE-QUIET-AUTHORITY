How These Pins Work — The Full Flow

  1. Upload pin image to Pinterest → set Destination URL to: https://beacons.ai/sanctuarygrace (or the direct assessment link)
  2. Woman clicks the pin → lands on your Beacons page / assessment
  3. She takes the 8-question assessment → enters name + email
  4. Beacons captures her → she enters your email sequence automatically
  5. Email 1 delivers her Silence Profile → Day 1 of the 7-Day Plan → link back to the app
  6. App deepens the relationship → she sees devotionals, books, Sacred Space products
  7. She purchases → Stripe → thank-you screen → purchase sequence in Beacons
